About Our Clinic

Where Expert Care Meets Modern Aesthetics

Sana Esthetics, located in the heart of Vaishali Nagar, Jaipur, is a premier aesthetic and cosmetic clinic founded with a mission to make high-quality treatments accessible and comfortable. We combine modern technology with a warm, patient-first approach.

Under the expert guidance of Dr. Nakul, we provide a full spectrum of aesthetic services — from hair transplant and laser treatments to dental implants and body contouring — all in a clean, hygienic, and welcoming environment.

  • Certified and experienced aesthetic professionals
  • Sterile, hygienic and modern facility
  • Transparent treatment plans and honest pricing

Our Services

Comprehensive Aesthetic Treatments

From hair restoration to body contouring, we offer everything you need for a confident new you — all under one roof.

Dental Implants

Permanent, natural-looking tooth replacements and complete dental care to restore your smile and confidence for life.

Facial Treatments

Dimple creation, skin rejuvenation, and advanced facial aesthetic procedures to enhance your natural beauty.

Hair Transplant

Advanced FUE & FUT hair transplant procedures to restore natural hairline and boost your confidence permanently.

Laser Hair Removal

Fat Loss / Liposuction

Medical body contouring and liposuction procedures to sculpt your ideal body shape safely and effectively.

Gynecomastia

Surgical correction of enlarged male breast tissue for a firmer, more masculine chest contour with lasting results.
